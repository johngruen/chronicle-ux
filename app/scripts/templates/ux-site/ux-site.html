§ What is Chronicle?

Chronicle is a tool to help you find all the best things you've seen and shared on the web.

We believe current tools for recovering previously viewed web-content are fundamentally broken. History and bookmarks are outmoded, clunky tools that are difficult to use effectively on a single device and nearly impossible to maintain across multiple devices.

Our user research tells us that users make due with a number of ad hoc methods for saving and recovering content. We believe we can do better.
